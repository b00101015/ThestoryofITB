Shuttle Bus Service

Shuttle bus pick-up points:
  • Coolmine Train Station
  • Blanchardstown Shopping Centre (Starbucks)
  • National Aquatic Centre
  • ITB campus

Fares
  • The cost is €1 per journey or €5 for a weekly ticket (which permits unlimited travel over a weekly period)
  • Passengers may pay with cash or they may use a Leap Card
  • Click here to purchase a student leap card

FAQs
  • Pay cash or use Leap Card or use your weekly ticket
  • Weekly tickets allows unlimited travel on the ITB Shuttle Bus
  • Holders of a valid Disability Bus Pass can use the ITB shuttle bus for free once they produce the pass to the driver.
  • The Shuttle Bus is FREE between the National Aquatic Centre (NAC) & ITB
  • Dublin Bus, Bus Eireann and a number of private operator’s (such as Express Bus service 801) operate in the area. Please contact the operator directly.
