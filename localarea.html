Historic map of Dublin.

In 1847, Griffiths valuation started to take place. This valuation was to map every area in Ireland. During this thime Buzzardstown and the rest of Ireland experienced the famine, Both the younger and older generations in the area died.



The affect of The Rising

In 1916, The Rising began throughout the country. It was most felt in Dublin. In this time railway lines were nearly destroyed which would have caused heartace throughout the city. During this time, Girls and Boys were still seperated in schools.

Throughout this time and just after The Rising, many young people from the surronding area emigrated to belfast and different countries in order to find work.


Grounds for ITB being built
